Born in Nova Scotia, my lust for trans-Atlantic (and Pacific!) adventures began early but didn't come into full fruition until after university. Graduating with a BCOMM in Global Business Management, I decided to move to Japan and become a kindergarten teacher (bet you didn't see that coming)! After 3.5 years and a quick stop in Thailand to market a pasta made from crickets (yes, the insect), I've landed in NYC, ready to take it head on. Currently I am studying graphic design and coding to become the best developer I can!
